Treatment Protocols & Research

Current specialist research emphasizes flexible splinting to prevent ankylosis. The graphs below compare outcomes based on splinting duration and type, derived from longitudinal studies on adult patients.

Gold Standard Protocol

  • ✔ Repositioning: Gentle digital pressure immediately.
  • ✔ Splinting: Flexible splint (wire/composite) for 2 weeks.
  • ✔ Diet: Soft diet for 14 days.
  • ✔ Follow-up: Pulp vitality testing at 2, 4, 12 weeks.
Research Note: Rigid splinting > 4 weeks significantly increases the risk of ankylosis in adults compared to children.
